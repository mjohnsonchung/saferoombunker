Our Story

THE QUIET
COMPANY

Saferoom & Bunker Co. was founded in Auckland in 2020 by a structural engineer who had spent the previous decade working on hardened government and defence infrastructure across the Pacific. The gap he identified was simple: the engineering knowledge required to build a proper underground structure existed, but there was no pathway for private individuals to access it.

The business launched with four production bunker plans. Within the first year, enquiries were arriving from the United States, Australia, and the United Kingdom. The product and the market had found each other immediately.

We have never sought publicity. Our clients do not discuss their projects publicly, and neither do we. What has grown the practice over six years is the quality of the engineering and the privacy with which we operate — two things that are not in tension. They are the same value, expressed differently.

Today we maintain a catalog of four production bunker plans, available worldwide under license. Beyond that, we offer two further tiers of engagement: a full turnkey build service where our New Zealand–based crew constructs on your existing property, and a complete acquisition service where we locate, assess, and purchase land on your behalf — with legal handling managed by our registered New Zealand solicitor — before our construction team takes over. Every engagement, at every tier, is covered by our standard NDA from first contact.

How We Work

ENGINEERING
FIRST. ALWAYS.

Engineer-Certified Plans

Every plan in our catalog carries a NZ Chartered Professional Engineer (CPEng) stamp. The drawings are not architectural concepts — they are construction documents. Your contractor can take them to ground.

Privacy as Standard

Every engagement is covered by NDA from first contact. We do not publish client names, project locations, or images of completed structures. Your plan, your builder, your bunker — and no one else's business.

Build Anywhere

Our plans have been built across 9 countries on 5 continents. The design is done in New Zealand; construction is yours to place. The included consultation adapts the engineering to your local soil, climate, and code environment.

New Zealand Based

Our offices are in Auckland and Wellington. Our design and engineering teams are entirely New Zealand-based, with access to some of the most rugged and varied terrain conditions on earth to test and validate structural performance.

Production Plans, Not Concepts

The four plans in our catalog were developed from direct construction experience across multiple countries and soil conditions. They are not theoretical — they are production documents refined by every build. The Citadel alone has been built in four countries. Each build informs the next revision of the drawings.

Your Contractor, Your Choice

We are not a construction company. We are a design and engineering firm. You take our plans to your own trusted contractor, or use our NZ build team if you're building here. You control the supply chain. You control who knows what.

Why We're Based Here

NEW ZEALAND
AS ADVANTAGE

New Zealand is not just where we work. It is the world's most strategically secure location for a survivable future — remote, politically stable, food and water self-sufficient, nuclear-free by law, and equipped with first-world infrastructure. But even if you're building in Montana, Texas, or the Scottish Highlands, you benefit from our New Zealand perspective: engineering underground structures in one of the planet's most geologically active, seismically challenging environments has made us very good at building things that last.
